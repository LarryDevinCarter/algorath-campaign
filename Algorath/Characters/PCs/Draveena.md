# Draveena

**Member of the Hunters** in the [[Factions/Gray Company|Gray Company]].

Formerly a Recruit of the Hunters. She blackmailed [[Characters/NPCs/Elijah Voss|Elijah Voss]] (then her superior) by threatening to reveal their affair to his wife unless he advanced her membership. As a direct result, she is now a full Member.
