# Vitae

**Vitae** was the court wizard of Good King [[King Omund|Omund]] of [[Vasloria]]. He created the Dragonborn to serve as the Dragon Phalanx. The ritual to create new dragonborn was lost with him. He was killed by the traitor [[Mandrake]].
