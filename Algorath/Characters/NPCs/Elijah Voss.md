# Elijah Voss

**First Hunter** of the [[Factions/Gray Company|Gray Company]].

Elijah Voss is the current First Hunter of the Gray Company. As the leader of the Hunters order, he oversees all monster extermination contracts, field operations, and the training of new recruits.

## Personality & Background
Elijah is a capable and respected leader, though he carries the weight of significant personal mistakes.

## Relationships
- He had an affair with [[Characters/PCs/Draveena|Draveena]] (then a Recruit of the Hunters).
- Draveena blackmailed him by threatening to reveal the affair to his wife unless he advanced her membership.
- As a direct result of the blackmail, Draveena is now a full Member of the Hunters.

Elijah remains married and works hard to maintain his position and the respect of his order despite the secret hanging over him.
