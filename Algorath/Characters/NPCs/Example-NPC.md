# Queen Elara Voss

**Ruler of the Kingdom of Aetheria and Archmage of the Silver Spire. Ambitious, visionary, and rumored to be haunted by visions of the empire's fall.**

## Basic Info
- **Race / Age**: Human (appears ~35, actual age unknown due to magic)
- **Class / Level**: Wizard (School of Divination) / ~15th level equivalent
- **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral (with Good tendencies)
- **Appearance**: Tall, silver-white hair often in elaborate braids, piercing blue eyes, always wears the Crown of Aetheria (a minor artifact).

## Personality & Motivations
- **Traits**: Charismatic in public, calculating in private. Values stability and magical progress above all.
- **Ideal**: "A unified realm under enlightened rule, where magic serves the people rather than the few."
- **Bond**: Deeply tied to the legacy of the old Aetherian Empire; fears repeating its collapse.
- **Flaw**: Paranoia about betrayal; has a network of spies and scrying orbs everywhere.

## Current Role in Campaign
- **Status with Party**: Initially patron or quest-giver. May become ally, rival, or tragic figure depending on player choices.
- **Current Goal**: Secure the kingdom against rising threats from the north (or whatever your plot is) and unlock ancient magics.
- **Secret**: She has been receiving prophetic dreams from a mysterious source that may be manipulating her.

## Relationships
- **With PCs**: Depends on their actions in Eldoria City. Respectful if they prove competent and loyal.
- **With Other NPCs**: Tense alliance with the Arcane Council; rivalry with certain noble houses.
- **Key Connections**: [[Characters/NPCs/Archmage-Sylvara]] (mentor/rival), [[Factions/Arcane-Council]]
