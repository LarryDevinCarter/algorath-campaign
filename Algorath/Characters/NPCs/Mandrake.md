# Mandrake

**Mandrake** (Bronze Dragonflight) betrayed the Dragonborn order. He killed **[[Vitae]]**, allowing [[Ajax|Ajax the Invincible]] to slay Good King [[King Omund|Omund]] and shatter the realm. This ended the Age of Law and plunged [[Vasloria]] into chaos.
