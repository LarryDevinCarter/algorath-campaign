# Lady of Brass

Former ruler of [[Alloy]]. She hired the [[Chain of Acheron]] to defeat the rebellion led by her general [[Xorn]]. Later, after [[Yxmic]] conquered the city in her absence, she dismissed the Chain, took refuge in her castle, and flew it out of the city into the plane of [[Algorath]].
