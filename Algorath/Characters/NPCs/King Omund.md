# King Omund

**King Omund**, known as The First Good King of [[Vasloria]]. His rule was one of peace and law. He ended conflicts between the dukes, created the **Dragon Phalanx** (dragonborn knights led by his wizard [[Vitae]]), and held back chaos until his betrayal and death at the hands of [[Ajax]] (known as Saint Ajax, Ajax the Immortal, the Immortal Emperor, or Ajax the Invincible).
