# Chain of Acheron

Mercenary company hired by the [[Lady of Brass]] to defeat the rebellion led by [[Xorn]] in [[Alloy]]. They fought in the Battle at the Doors of Morning. Later dismissed by the Lady after [[Yxmic]]'s conquest.
