# The Athenaeum

The largest Institute of higher learning in all of [[Motavos]]. It is located in [[Locations/Settlements/Piata|Piata]]. The Institute is so large that its grounds take up the northern third of the city. Its influence is so great that high-ranking members of the Institute often also sit in positions of power for the city itself.

There is a current ongoing situation where monstrosities have started infiltrating the grounds of the institution. One student at the school is dead, and an instructor was maimed. The basement level of the institution's largest building has been sealed off. This has slowed down the occurrences significantly, but occasionally these monstrosities find a way to appear on the grounds of the Institute anyway. They have contracted with the [[Factions/Gray Company|Gray Company]] to eliminate the threat.

Notable members include the archaeologist [[Characters/NPCs/Professor Holt|Professor Holt]], who is currently missing. He was last known to be on an expedition to Zema and the veil of first light. Maki's current contract brings her here to reconnect with him.
