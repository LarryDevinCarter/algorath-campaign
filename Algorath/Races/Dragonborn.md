# Dragonborn

Dragonborn were not truly born but were created by **[[Vitae]]**, the court wizard of **[[King Omund]]** of [[Vasloria]], to be his Dragon Phalanx.

At their height there were nearly 2,000 dragonborn. Most have been hunted down and killed by [[Ajax]] and his forces. It is well known even in areas outside Ajax's domain that a bounty is on the head of all remaining dragonborn.

There were 10 orders of dragonborn matching the scales of the knights and 10 metallic/chromatic dragonflights whose blood the dragonborn were infused with. The ritual to create new dragonborn was lost with [[Vitae]], but dragonborn can breed although their offspring show atypical traits such as feathers and have a low rate of survival.

They are known as the ideal knights, protecting the weak, dispensing justice, and elite fighters.

## Dragonflights

- **Black Dragonflight (The Oracles)**: Seers with prescience, mind projection, and deception-piercing abilities via rituals and drugs. Last Captain: Raijorozaan.
- **Blue Dragonflight (The Sentinels)**: Bodyguards and defenders; stubborn and loyal. One fortress was [[Castle Rend]] (near [[Gravesford]]). Last Captain: Ahrijiinad.
- **Red Dragonflight (The Priests)**: Noble flight devoted to the god [[Adun]] (Strength); haughty and often prioritized divine will. Absent during the final betrayal. Last Captain: Korviskoor.
- **White Dragonflight (The Censors)**: Stealthy questing knights who pursued justice relentlessly. Last Captain: Bakadrazaar.
- **Bronze Dragonflight (The Zealots)**: Fanatic and devoted; inspired (or drove) reckless heroism. Last Captain: Ziirojian. (Notable traitor: [[Mandrake]].)
- **Copper Dragonflight (The Stalwart)**: Invincible defenders who bolstered armies. Last Captain: Kivaaj.
- **Green Dragonflight (The Saboteurs)**: Smallest; focused on harassment and battlefield disruption. Last Captain: Hijaanistak.
- **Gold, Silver, Brass Dragonflights**: Very rare and had purposes kept secret.

## Notable Members

- **Mandrake** (Bronze Dragonflight): Betrayed the order. He killed [[Vitae]], allowing [[Ajax|Ajax the Invincible]] to slay Good King [[King Omund|Omund]] and shatter the realm. This ended the Age of Law and plunged [[Vasloria]] into chaos.
