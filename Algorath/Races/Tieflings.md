# Tieflings

Tieflings are the opposite of [[Aasimar]], born when a woman has a relationship with a fiend or demon influence taints a child within the womb. Warlocks who have pacts with demons often give birth to Tieflings. They are typically shunned by most societies as they are seen as inherently evil. While they are connected to their fiendish parent in some way, their choices are their own.
