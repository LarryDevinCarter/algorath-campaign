# Gnome

Gnomes are excellent scholars that remember when every Gnome had a magical connection to the Weave. And they want that back. Living communally in the Gnome Hole (giant inverted underground compound). All their efforts are focused on one goal: learning how to bring magic back to the Gnomish people.
