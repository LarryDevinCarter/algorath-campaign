# Dwarf

Dwarves are short, proud, and stocky. The dwarves of [[Algorath]] mostly stay in their underground cities and keep to themselves, although some clans abide in the hills, preferring animal husbandry to mining.

The dwarves were one of the first intelligent mortal creatures in [[Algorath]], and the second to found a civilization. The dwarves even invented time itself, as the slow-aging elves had no use for such a concept.

Dwarves tend to be easily persuaded to worship lower gods than that of [[Pelor]] and his god-seed children.

## Subgroups and Worship
- **Mountain/Mine Dwarves**: Believe themselves to be created by their god [[Grumbar]], the god of the earth, who they say built the world.
- **Deep Dwarves**: Worship various fire elemental gods and old gods.
- **Hill Dwarves**: Worship [[Chauntea]] (a god-seed of [[Pelor]], goddess of agriculture, growth, and the land/plane of [[Algorath]] itself).

## History and Civilizations
There were once many dwarven civilizations. After the fall of the [[Steel Dwarves of Kalas Mithral]] to the armies of the [[Army of Night]] and the [[Astral Celestials]], there are only 3 civilizations remaining:
- **Stone Dwarves**: Residing in the [[Hanging City of Kenkarra]], in the [[Granite Mountains]].
- **Hill Dwarves**: Residing in [[Blackpeak]].
- **Deep Dwarves**: That live far underground.

With the rise of [[Ajax the Invincible]], the Wood Elves of the [[Viridian Wode]] betrayed the stone dwarves by revealing the secret entrance to [[Kenkarra]] to [[Ajax]]. The Stone Dwarves were defeated but bowed to [[Ajax]], and were allowed to continue to govern themselves if they gave slaves to [[Ajax]] in tithe. For this reason, dwarves now despise elves for their treachery, and they are themselves despised by humans as slavers.

[[Ajax]] was never able to find the entrance to the Deep Dwarves' lair. They started sending him slaves voluntarily to prevent his ire.

The Hill Dwarves of [[Blackpeak]] were most recently conquered, themselves being betrayed by the high elves. They now supply their prize griffons to [[Ajax]] for his [[Hawklords]].

Many Dwarven clans have been conquered by [[Ajax]]. One of Ajax's teachings is that of human superiority. All other races must submit and may have been taken as slaves.

The Dwarves have been forced (some willingly and some not) to become slavers for Ajax. Capturing and offering slaves to Ajax to keep some manner of peace with him.

Other clans breed Griffons as mounts for Ajax's elite shock troopers, the Hawklords.

Yet other dwarves still live deep underground worshipping evil gods of fire and madness.

This has led most people of [[Motavos]] to be hateful and distrustful of Dwarves in general.
