# Orc

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 2, and your Intelligence score is reduced by 2.

**Age.** Orcs reach adulthood at age 12 and live up to 50 years.

**Alignment.** Orcs are usually chaotic evil.

**Size.** Medium.

**Speed.** 30 feet.

**Darkvision.** You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Aggressive.** As a bonus action, you can move up to your speed toward an enemy that you can see or hear. You must end this move closer to the enemy than you started.

**Powerful Build.** You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Orc.

**Subrace.** Orcs are divided into four distinct groups. Choose one of the following subraces.

## Mountain Orc (Traditional cave- and camp-dwelling orcs)
**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Menacing.** You are proficient in the Intimidation skill.

## Woodland Orc (Honor-bound, community-focused forest orcs)
**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Challenge.** When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with a melee attack on your turn, you can use your reaction to move up to half your speed toward another enemy and make a single weapon attack against it. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

## Fiend-Touched Orc (Fiend-infected shock troopers)
**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Fiendish Rage.** When you take damage, you can use your reaction to gain temporary hit points equal to your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier, and your next weapon attack deals an extra 1d6 fire damage. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

## Deep Orc (Underground orcs who worship fire elemental gods and the old gods)
**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Void Scarred.** You have resistance to fire damage. In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.
