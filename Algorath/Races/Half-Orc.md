# Half-Orc

Half-Orcs, products of one of the most vile evils (Orcs forcing themselves on human women), share the traits of both parents. Not accepted in Orc tribes, barely tolerated in human society. Half-Orcs live a hard life, but their strength does help them find their place in the world.
