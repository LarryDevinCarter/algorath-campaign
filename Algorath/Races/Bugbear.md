# Bugbear

In this world, bugbears are **true neutral** and have average intelligence, comparable to that of a human.

## Society
As a society of traders and tradesmen, they do not speak Goblin (or share any kinship with goblins) but speak Common, Dwarvish, and Gnomish.

Bugbears still keep their traditional brutish build and strength, preferring to attack with large titan-steel weapons. Large battle axes are a common weapon of choice for them.

They raise basilisks the way humans raise cows for meat.

They control the **Titan-steel Guild**; they guard the secret of making titan-steel, the strongest metal known to any race.

They value family highly, and many bugbears are in trades shared by their father and passed down by their father's father.
