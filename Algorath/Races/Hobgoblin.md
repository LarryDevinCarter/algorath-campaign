# Hobgoblin

Hobgoblins are born of pure evil. Goblins, knowing they need good leaders for the tribe to succeed, often capture human women to enslave and breed. The byproduct is a Hobgoblin. Larger, stronger, and smarter than other Goblins. They prefer Goblin societies to the human ones. Hobgoblins are unable to reproduce themselves, forcing the Goblin tribe to produce another once their Hobgoblin dies.
