# Quintessence

Quintessence is an elemental plane in our cosmology. Its capital city is [[Alloy]].

It was formed when the [[Elemental Gods]] from each of the elemental planes of existence banded together to create a new plane that was hidden from [[Pelor]] and his children.

The [[Elemental Gods]] themselves were born of the energy of the elemental planes and are considered much weaker than the true gods, such as [[Pelor]], but far superior to any being born of the material plane.

Because this plane of existence is only connected to the four elemental planes, the only way to get there is to first travel to one of the elemental planes. Also, the only magic that works is elemental magic.
