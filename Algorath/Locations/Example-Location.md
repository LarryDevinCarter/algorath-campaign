# Eldoria City

**The gleaming capital of the Kingdom of Aetheria, known for its silver spires and bustling arcane markets.**

## Overview
Eldoria City sits at the confluence of the Silver River and the ancient ley lines of the old empire. It is a hub of trade, magic, and political power. Population ~120,000. Ruled by Queen Elara Voss and the Arcane Council.

## Key Districts
- **The Spire District**: Seat of government and high magic academies. Home to the Royal University of Arcane Arts.
- **Market Ward**: Vibrant bazaar where exotic goods and minor magic items are traded. Watch for pickpockets and guild intrigues.
- **The Undercity**: Sprawling sewers and forgotten catacombs beneath the city. Home to thieves' guilds, smugglers, and ancient secrets.

## Plot Hooks
- The party is hired to investigate strange disappearances in the Undercity.
- A rival noble house seeks to sabotage the Queen's upcoming coronation anniversary.
- Rumors of an ancient artifact hidden in the old university vaults.
