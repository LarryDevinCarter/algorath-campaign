# Palmar

**Palmar** was one of the three great continents. It was destroyed approximately 2,000 years ago during the Calamity by a cult of Therizdun, which also shattered the first of the three great seals binding Therizdun.

Chunks of Palmar rained down on Motavos, reshaping its geography into peninsulas, gulfs, a large inland lake, and islands.
