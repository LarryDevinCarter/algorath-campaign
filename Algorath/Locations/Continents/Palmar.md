# Palmar

**Palmar** was one of the three great continents built atop the seals.

Approximately 2,000 years ago, a cult of [[Therizdun]] triggered a cataclysm that destroyed the continent of [[Palmar]] and shattered the first of the three great seals binding [[Therizdun]]. This event is known as the [[Calamity]]. Only two seals remain intact.

The destruction sent large chunks of land flying across the sea. As [[Motavos]] was the closest of the other two continents, these chunks rained down on Motavos, having devastating effects on the population and permanently reshaping the land into peninsulas, gulfs, a large inland lake, and islands (11 sections total, 4 of which are island-based).
