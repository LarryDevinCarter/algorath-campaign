# Alloy

Alloy, also known as the Burning City and the City of Brass, is the capital city of the elemental plane of [[Quintessence]].

## History

Until recently, Alloy was ruled by the [[Lady of Brass]]. Her rule was threatened when her general, [[Xorn]] betrayed her and turned her armies against her. In response, she hired the mercenary company known as the [[Chain of Acheron]] to put down the rebellion. The Chain fought Xorn's forces for three days in the Battle at the Doors of Morning, which ended with Xorn defeated and the rebels having surrendered.

Three years later, while the Lady of Brass and the Chain were in the Shadowfell, a Fire Dwarf called [[Yxmic]] with an army of djinn and efreet assaulted and conquered the city. Yxmic enacted a pogrom in the city, killing all those not native to the plane. More than two million of the city's inhabitants fled, reducing its population to a few hundred thousand. The Lady of Brass, upon seeing what had happened in her absence, dismissed the Chain and took refuge in her own castle, whose fortifications Yxmic had failed to penetrate, and flew the castle out of the city and into the plane of [[Algorath]].

Some time in the past the Lady of Brass was searching as the leader of the city. The city is now ruled by [[Yxmic]], a fire dwarf.
