# Kenkarra

The [[Hanging City of Kenkarra]], in the [[Granite Mountains]]. Home to the Stone Dwarves.

The Wood Elves of the [[Viridian Wode]] betrayed the stone dwarves by revealing the secret entrance to [[Kenkarra]] to [[Ajax the Invincible]]. The Stone Dwarves were defeated but allowed to continue governing themselves in exchange for slaves given to [[Ajax]] in tithe.
