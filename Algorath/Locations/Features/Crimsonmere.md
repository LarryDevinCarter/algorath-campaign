# Crimsonmere

**Crimsonmere** is a large sea located directly east of the [[Crimson-Span|Crimson Span]] in the south-by-southeastern region of [[High Piedmont]].

It has only two locations suitable for making port on the coast of High Piedmont. The rest of the coast is too jagged or features high bluffs that prevent ship landings and make invasion difficult.

It lies to the northeast of the [[Westerlands]], acting with the [[Ashenfang Mountains]] to separate the Westerlands from [[High Piedmont]].
