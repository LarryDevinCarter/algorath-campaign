# The Shroud

The **Shroud** is the native term for the ever-present fog over the ocean that makes travel by ocean impossible. It surrounds the northernmost coast of [[High Piedmont]].

Sailing too close risks smashing against rocks; going too far into the Shroud causes loss of bearings and disappearance.
