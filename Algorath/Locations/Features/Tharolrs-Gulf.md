# Tharolr's Gulf

**Tharolr's Gulf** lies to the east of [[High Piedmont]]. There are no ports on the eastern shores due to high cliffs above the sea.

Sailing the gulf to reach High Piedmont requires tracing the shores tightly until reaching the beaches of the northern coast, complicated by the [[Shroud]].
