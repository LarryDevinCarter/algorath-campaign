# Westerlands

The **Westerlands** is a region located directly south of [[High Piedmont]] and northwest of [[Ithenvale]].

## Geography

- The **[[Ashenfang Mountains]]** lie to its north.
- **[[Crimsonmere]]** lies to its northeast; these features act to separate it from [[High Piedmont]].
- To the southeast is **[[The Golden Maw]]**, a quicksand sea.
- A yet unnamed sea to the south separates it from [[Ithenvale]].

## Settlements

- It contains the city of [[Kadurûn]].

## Notable Locations

- **[[Barad Bane]]**, Ziothar's fortress, is located here.
