# Azure Marches

The **Azure Marches** is the region to the direct east of [[Heartlands]], on the other side of the large inland lake.

To its northeast, [[The Wyrmsea]] (the largest quicksand sea on [[Motavos]]) creates a natural barrier separating it from [[The Eastern Reach]].
