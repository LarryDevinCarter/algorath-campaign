# Vespera

**Vespera** is a large island located to the far southeast of [[Motavos]]. It is one of the 11 major sections of the continent, four of which are island-based.

Vespera serves as the ancestral homeland of the dark elves. Its southern half is defined by a network of rivers and streams blessed by [[Selûne]], the Moon Goddess worshipped devoutly by the dark elves of the isle. Dark elves who drink from these sacred waters find their innate hunger for magical connection quenched, often awakening or strengthening an affinity for moon-based magics. The region is known for its priests of Selûne, druids who frequently wild shape into owls or shadow cats, and powerful lunar sorcerers who draw directly from the goddess’s light.
