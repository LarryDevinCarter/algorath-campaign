# Jade Peninsula

The **Jade Peninsula** is directly south of [[The Eastern Reach]]. It was once part of the kingdom of [[Vasloria]], but is now under the control of [[Saint Ajax]].

## Notable Places
- **Terminus**: A city on the peninsula.
- **Ladea's Tower**: The tower of Ladea, a powerful wizard from a long-forgotten era. It still stands as if immune to the ages.

Far to the south of The Jade Peninsula is [[The Isle of Winds]].
