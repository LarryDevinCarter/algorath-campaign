# Isle of Winds

The **Isle of Winds** was also part of the kingdom of [[Vasloria]] and is now under the control of [[Saint Ajax]].

## Settlements
- **Torinco** (often called the Stepstone City or the City of Steps): The most notable settlement. The city is built on naturally occurring concentric plateaus.
