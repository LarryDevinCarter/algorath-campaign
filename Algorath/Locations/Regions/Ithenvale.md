# Ithenvale

**Ithenvale** is the region connected to [[High Piedmont]] by the massive [[Crimson-Span|Crimson Span]] bridge, which stands 11,000 feet above the sea.

It is one of the areas linked by this wonder of the world built by a long-forgotten society.
