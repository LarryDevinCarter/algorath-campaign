# Heartlands

The **Heartlands** is a region of the mainland of [[Motavos]] near the center of the map. It runs from just south of [[Zema]], includes [[Molcrum]] (a large Bugbear village), [[Krupmere]] (a city on the coast of the large inland lake in the middle of the continent), and the [[Jagged Peninsula]] (a long peninsula covered in large mountains, almost completely uninhabited due to the rough terrain) to the northeast.

To the direct east, on the other side of the large inland lake, lies [[Azure Marches]].

## Settlements
- **[[Molcrum]]**: A large [[Bugbear]] village.
- **[[Krupmere]]**: A city on the coast of the large inland lake.

## Features
- **[[Jagged Peninsula]]**: A long peninsula covered in large mountains, almost completely uninhabited due to the rough terrain.
