# Motavos

**The fractured heart of the known world, a vast and broken land where the scars of the Calamity still bleed into the earth.**

## Overview
Motavos is the primary continent of the current campaign. Once more contiguous and verdant, its geography was violently reshaped ~2,000 years ago during the destruction of [[Palmar]]. Massive fragments of the doomed continent rained down upon Motavos, carving deep impacts that created a labyrinth of peninsulas, gulfs, a great inland lake, and countless islands.

The continent is divided into **11 major sections**, four of which are island chains or large islands. It stretches across diverse biomes, though much of its former lushness is fading.

## Geography & Regions
- **High Piedmont** — Far northeast; rugged highlands and fortified settlements.
- **Vespera** — Large island in the far southeast; mysterious and storm-lashed.
- **The Shattered Coasts** — Network of peninsulas and gulfs formed by the meteor-like impacts.
- **The Inland Sea** — Vast central lake, its waters said to hide submerged ruins and strange currents.
- **Quicksand Seas** — Treacherous expanses where the land itself devours travelers.
- **Withering Heartlands** — Once-fertile plains now giving way to scrub, desert patches, and encroaching wasteland.

## Ecology & Environment
Long ago, the oily **backstone temples** maintained by the faithful of [[Pelor]] kept the land supernaturally green and bountiful. As the temples fell into disrepair and the number of Pelor's followers dwindled, this unnatural vitality has receded. Deserts spread, quicksand fields multiply, and strange mutations appear in the flora and fauna.

## History
- Pre-Calamity: Lush, temple-blessed paradise.
- The Calamity (~2000 years ago): Bombardment by Palmar fragments reshaped the land, devastated populations, and weakened the first seal.
- Present: Slow ecological decline as ancient stabilizing magic fades.

## Plot Hooks
- Expeditions into impact craters uncover ancient Palmar artifacts or dormant eldritch corruption.
- A dying backstone temple requires rare reagents or lost rituals to reactivate.
- Rival factions fight over control of the Inland Sea's fertile shores as resources grow scarce.
- Cultists of [[Therizdun]] seek to accelerate the land's decay to weaken the remaining seals.

## Connections
- Linked to [[Palmar]] via ruins and meteor debris.
- Trade and conflict with [[Dezoris]].
- Home to many [[Pelor]] worshipers and their fading temples.

## DM Notes
The impacts left behind not only physical scars but pockets of unstable planar energy. Some craters serve as gateways to the Feywild or Shadowfell, or worse—direct leaks from the Void.

*Adventure potential: Player characters could be tasked with restoring a key temple or investigating why certain impact zones are spawning monstrous hybrids.*
