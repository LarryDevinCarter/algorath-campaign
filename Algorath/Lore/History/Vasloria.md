# Vasloria

The kingdom of **Vasloria** once included all of the regions of [[The Eastern Reach]], [[The Jade Peninsula]], [[The Isle of Winds]], and [[The Dusk Isles]]. Its center of power was in [[The Eastern Reach]].

## Rulers
Ruled by **[[King Omund]]** (known as The First Good King of Vasloria). His rule was one of peace and law. He ended conflicts between the dukes, created the **Dragon Phalanx** (dragonborn knights led by his wizard [[Vitae]]), and held back chaos until his betrayal and death at the hands of [[Ajax]].
