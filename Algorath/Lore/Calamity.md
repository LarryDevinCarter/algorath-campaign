# The Calamity

Approximately 2,000 years ago, a cult of [[Therizdun]] triggered a cataclysm that destroyed the continent of [[Palmar]] and shattered the first of the three great seals binding [[Therizdun]].

Only two seals remain intact, and the god-seed continues its slow maturation as an ongoing threat.

This event had profound effects on [[Motavos]], reshaping its geography through falling debris.
