# Calendar of Algorath

The calendar divides the year into four seasons with three months each.

## Spring
- **Newgrowth** (31 days)
- **Greenveil** (30 days)
- **Blossom** (31 days)

## Summer
- **Highsun** (30 days)
- **Highsummer** (31 days)
- **Harvesteve** (31 days)

## Autumn
- **Goldfall** (30 days)
- **Ashenwind** (31 days)
- **Dusk** (30 days)

## Winter
- **Frostveil** (31 days)
- **Ironcold** (30 days)
- **Longnight** (31 days)

## Game Start
The campaign begins on **29 [[Highsummer]], 2284 AW**.
