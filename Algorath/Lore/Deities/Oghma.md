# Oghma

One of the later [[Pelor|god-seeds]] of [[Pelor]]. Not quite driven to madness, but driven to indifference. He is the patron God of learning and knowledge.

Hidden somewhere on [[Locations/Continents/Motavos|Motavos]] is a hidden library temple that has existed before the mortal races or any written languages. Those who wish to gain his favor must add new knowledge to this library.
