# Therizdun

**The Destroyer**, born from the splitting of the One. Banished and sealed beneath Algorath by [[Pelor]] after their primordial war.

Cults devoted to him seek to shatter the remaining seals and unleash his return, which would unmake creation.

**Current Status**: Bound, with two seals remaining after the destruction of [[Palmar]].
